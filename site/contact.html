Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms does Valtronics support?

+

Valtronics supports ESP32, Arduino (Uno, Mega, and compatible boards), and STM32 microcontrollers. Our unified API allows you to write code once and deploy across all supported platforms.

How do I get started with Valtronics?

+

Start by reading our getting started guide in the documentation. Install the required development tools, set up your first device, and follow our step-by-step tutorials. We also offer comprehensive examples and a support team to help you get up and running quickly.

What kind of sensors can I connect?

+

Valtronics supports a wide range of sensors including temperature (DHT22, DS18B20, BME280), humidity, pressure, air quality (PMS5003, MQ-135), vibration (ADXL345), and industrial sensors. We support I2C, SPI, UART, and analog sensor interfaces.

Is there a free tier available?

+

Yes! We offer a free tier that includes up to 5 devices, basic analytics, and community support. Paid tiers are available for larger deployments with advanced features, priority support, and enterprise-grade security.

How secure is the Valtronics platform?

+

Security is our top priority. We implement end-to-end encryption, secure boot, TLS/SSL communication, hardware encryption support, and industrial-grade authentication protocols. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.

Can I integrate Valtronics with existing systems?

+

Absolutely! Valtronics supports multiple communication protocols including MQTT, HTTP/HTTPS, Modbus, LoRa, and CAN bus. Our RESTful API and WebSocket interfaces make it easy to integrate with existing IoT platforms, SCADA systems, and enterprise applications.
